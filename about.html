Who am I?

I'm currently pursing a PhD in Technology and Social Behavior (TSB) at Northwestern University in Chicagoland. My Bachelor's of Science is from the the University of Washington in Human-Centered Design and Engineering (HCDE) with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Between undergrad and grad school I worked in industry for a while. See my resume for more info!

I am a tech, sci-fi, and fantasy geek and love when these categories intertwine. Think speculative science fiction, immersive gaming, cool gadgets, and technological politics and you begin to understand what excites me about what I do.

I am originally from New York City but I have also lived in Canada, England, and Seattle before moving to Chicagoland.
