About Me

Who am I?:

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Washington, currently majoring in Human-Centered Design (HCDE) and Engineering with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)

I am a tech, sci-fi, and fantasy geek and love when these categories intertwine. Think speculative science fiction, immersive gaming, cool gadgets, and technological politics and you begin to understand what excites me about my field of study.

I am originally from New York City but I have also lived in Canada and England before coming to Seattle. During that time, I studied economics with a focus on international development and worked for many years as a server and bartender.
